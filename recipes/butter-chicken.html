Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken traditionally known as Murgh Makhni is an Indian Dish originatiing in Delhi. It is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter sauce. It's sauce is known for its rich texture. It is similar to Chicken Tikka Masala, which uses a tomato paste.The curry was developed at the Moti Mahal restaurant in the Daryaganj neighbourhood of Old Delhi in the 1950s, after the partition of India by Kundan Lal Jaggi and Kundan Lal Gujral,who were both Punjabi refugees from Peshawar.

For its preparation Chicken is marinated for several hours in a mixture of lemon juice, dahi (yogurt), Kashmiri red chili, salt, garam masala, ginger paste, and garlic paste. The marinated chicken is cooked in a tandoor (traditional clay oven), but may be grilled, oven-roasted, or pan-fried. It is served in a mild curry sauce that includes butter.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast slice to cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Chilly Powder - 2 Teaspoons
